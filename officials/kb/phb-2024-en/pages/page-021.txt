Saving Throws. A saving throw is used to see
whether a creature avoids or resists something. When the chest releases toxic gas,
Phillip's and Amy's characters make Constitution saving throws. Constitution is one
of the six abilities that measure creatures'
capabilities, and it has to do with health and
stamina. When they fail their saves, the gas
causes them to have the Paralyzed condition. See "Conditions" later in this chapter
for more information.
